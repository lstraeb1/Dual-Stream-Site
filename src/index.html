A Little About Us

Dual Stream Web Designs primarily seeks to help small businesses boost their online presence through a high quality, hand-coded website. Our goal is not only to make a visually appealing website for you and your business, but also make it high performing to boost search engine rankings, and ultimately revenue!

More About Us

Mobile First

The majority of web browsing is done on mobile devices today. which is why we code all of our websites to be optimized on mobile and scale up to fit on tablets and laptops.

Pagespeed Optimized

A high performing website is the key to success in search engine rankings. Our websites are guaranteed to score 98-100 in Performance, SEO, Accessibility, and Best Practices.

Custom Made

What's the point of having your own website if it doesn't fit your brand identity? Our websites are custom-made to perfectly reflect your business values and culture.

How it Works

Plans start at an affordable $150/month for a standard custom 5-page website with a 12 Month contract minimum. We'll start by going over the design before any code is written. Once we make any changes and you approve it, we can get started on the final product. From there, It's as simple as buying a domain and getting the website live!.
